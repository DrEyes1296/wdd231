NP Membership (No Fee)

This membership is exclusively for **Non-Profit Organizations**.

Benefits Include:

  • Inclusion in Chamber Directory
  • Access to Monthly Newsletter
  • Non-Profit Networking Events
×

Bronze Membership ($50/month)

Ideal for small businesses just starting out.

Benefits Include:

  • All NP benefits
  • Basic listing in Chamber Directory
  • Discounts on Chamber Event Tickets
  • Access to basic business training workshops
×

Silver Membership ($100/month)

A step up for growing businesses with increased visibility.

Benefits Include:

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Priority placement in Chamber Directory
  • **One-time** Home Page Spotlight consideration per year
  • 25% discount on Event Booth space
  • Invitations to special leadership events
×

Gold Membership ($250/month)

The premium level with maximum benefits and advertising.

Benefits Include:

  • All Silver benefits
  • **Guaranteed** Home Page Spotlight consideration (up to 3 times/year)
  • Free admission to all training workshops
  • Opportunity to host a Chamber event
  • Top tier placement in all print and digital materials
